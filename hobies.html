My Hobbies

I have a deep passion for reading novels, especially on Wattpad. My favorite genres are romance, action, and thriller. When it comes to horror novels, I prefer to buy physical books instead of reading them online. Aside from reading, I enjoy watching Kdramas, particularly in the romance, horror, and thriller genres, as they bring an exciting mix of emotions and suspense. I also love watching movies, but not just Korean films. I enjoy films from all around the world, appreciating the diverse storytelling styles.
