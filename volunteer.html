About the Event

The Penang Bridge International Marathon (PBIM) is an annual event where participants from around the globe gather to run and celebrate fitness and community. As a volunteer, I had the privilege of assisting participants, coordinating logistics, and ensuring the smooth operation of the event. Our team is involved in taking care of the water station.It was a rewarding experience that allowed me to connect with diverse individuals and contribute to an international event.
